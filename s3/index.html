How to create Excel Data Sheet

Step 1:
Open File.xlsx (on Dekstop)
Step 2:
paste in Control Values (windtunnel with no car) into the green box on the template sheet. (click in top cornor cell)
Step 3:
paste in Car Values (windtunnel with car) into the Blue box on the template sheet. (click in top cornor cell of blue box)
Step 4:
Upload to server
